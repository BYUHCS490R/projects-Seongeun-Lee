The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?
It has the address or the link where your data in form goes.

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?
If you use "get," then your data in form will show up in the address, but "post" will hide it. "Get" is used for reading or serching, and "post" is used for updates or changes.
