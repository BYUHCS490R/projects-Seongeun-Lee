1. Block tags create a new box, but inline tags don't do it.
For example, I used 'br' tag for this sentence, and this is still in the same box, unlike the next question or the paragraph.

2. The margin is the space between the box and the edge of the page or the next box. In short, the space outside of the box is the margin. The border is the edge of the box, and the padding is the space between border and the text or the content.

3. The examples of the measuring units are px and %.

4. I would use a figure tag to make a group of multiple things like image and description together.
