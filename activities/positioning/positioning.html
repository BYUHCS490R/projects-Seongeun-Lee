What is normal flow in HTML?

Flow is the order and the way that contents are placed in the main. In the normal flow, contents are displayed based on the order of the tag you write. If it is an inline tag, the line won't break.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

When you make the tag float, other box like paragraph can come next, horizontally. But sometime you want to decide how many boxes beside the tag, in that case, you should clear it.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

If the position is fixed, it doesn't move at all even when you scroll down the screen. If the position is absolute, it ignores the document flow and is positioned based on the number of px. If the position is relative, the flow doesn't change, so the boxes above or below remain same.
