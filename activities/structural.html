Structural Tags

1. What goes in the header?
Usually, the name of the company or of the page goes in the header. It could be the logo.

2. What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?
The Nav has links to go to other pages which users often look for. The Nav can go anywhere, however, it usually goes in, above, or below the header for. The nav could contain another website but it is not common because people do not want users to leave their website. If the other websites are from the same company, they would have it.

3. What goes in Main?
All the information/texts and links in the page except for navigation or footer go here.

4. What goes in the Footer?
It is located at the bottom of the page. Something important but not for user convenience like copyright goes here.
