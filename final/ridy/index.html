RIDY is a ride-sharing service that helps passengers save time and money while enabling drivers to earn extra income.

Drivers share their departure times and destinations, and passengers pay a fee to join the ride, which compensates the driver and covers the platform's costs.

Check Our Unique Features

  • GPS-based notification system
  • Guardian registration service

You will receive notifications if the driver deviates from the expected route. You can also register family members or friends to be notified if you move away from your destination or stay in the same place for an extended period of time.

We value our passengers' safety

Drivers must complete all required validation procedures to be registered, just like in other ride-sharing apps.
