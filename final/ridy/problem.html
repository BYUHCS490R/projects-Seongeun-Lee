According to BYUH Quick Facts 2025-2026, more than half of BYUH students are part of the IWORK program. Because IWORK students need special permission to own a car, many of them rely on slow public transportation or pay high rental costs to get around.

Traveling is Inconvenient and Expensive!

The nearest car-rental option, Mahalo Car Share in Hau'ula, is expensive and even charges an additional bus fee. As a result, many students borrow friends' cars without proper insurance. Others take the bus, which stops frequently and makes it difficult to carry groceries or other items.
